Audio Recording

Obtaining high quality audio inside our world class facility is by far our core responsibility! For over a decade, Distinct Music Productions has professionally recorded the music of gospel singers, jazz and rock bands, brass & acoustic instrumentalists, voiceover clients and many more.

Music Production & Song Writing

When an artist is seeking guidance in the creation of their sound, we offer the assistance of one of our highly skilled music producers. Our producers can take your idea and turn it into a meticulously written song with lyrics, forming a good music.

Mixing & Mastering

At Distinct Music Productions, we have highly advanced mix engineers whose skills lie in mixing and mastering inside dedicated post-production rooms that allow for a swift workflow to maximize the quality of your final track. 

Session Musicians

If required, we provide exceptional vocals and musicians for every session being booked for. Many artists without bands utilize the brilliance of our exceptional session musicians to convey their musical vision in a recording.

Music Production Academy

This is designed for beginners or intermediate to develop abilities as a competent and successful music producers. We introduce fundamental Pro Tools concepts and principles for beginners to learn how to build sessions for multi-track recordings of live audio, midi sequences and virtual instruments.
